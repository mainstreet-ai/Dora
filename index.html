How it works

From request to your front door.

First

Tell Dora what you want

Send a quick request below — your color palette, a holiday or season, or a wreath from the gallery you'd like matched. The more detail, the better the fit.

Then

She hand-ties it

Every wreath is built fresh, by hand, around a grapevine or wire base — no two turn out identical. Most requests are ready within about a week.

Finally

Pick it up, ready to hang

Dora will confirm pickup details with you directly once it's finished. Local pickup in Jacksonville, FL.

About Dora

Twenty years of wreaths, one ribbon bow at a time.

Dora Fennell has been designing and hand-tying wreaths in Jacksonville for two decades — long enough to have a process, an eye for proportion, and a drawer full of ribbon she's still excited to use.

Every piece is built from scratch for the person who ordered it. No pre-made stock, no two exactly alike. If you've got a vision — a color you love, a holiday you decorate hard for, or just "something like that one, but more pink" — that's exactly the kind of request she likes best.
